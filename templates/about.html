The cinema is there to heighten the imagination; I have always tried to make sure it does so.
Kenn Adam

Seeing myself dedicated to the story that is about to be told on screen, I always try first to understand their characters. I try to understand where they are coming from and what has made them the way they seem to be at the moment I meet them. I belief that there lays some magic in all stories. Trying to feel its need and its distortion, I want to create worlds that make the invisible to be seen.
